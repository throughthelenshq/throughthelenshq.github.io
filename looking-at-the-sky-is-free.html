Looking at the Sky is Free

A reminder that the simplest things in life are often the easiest to overlook.

The Story

Sometimes we spend so much time looking at our phones, worrying about tomorrow, or rushing through the day that we forget to simply look up.

As I looked toward the sky, a helicopter happened to pass above the building. The clean composition and the empty space around it made the moment feel peaceful rather than busy.

This photograph reminds me that nature, the sky, and the little moments around us are free. We just have to slow down enough to notice them.
