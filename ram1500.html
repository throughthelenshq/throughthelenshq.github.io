RAM 1500

Strength, simplicity, and bold design.

The Story

Some vehicles stand out without trying.

The RAM 1500 immediately caught my attention because of its bold stance and clean lines. Rather than focusing on every detail, I wanted to create a simple composition that let the truck speak for itself.

Sometimes the strongest photographs come from slowing down and appreciating everyday scenes from a different perspective.
